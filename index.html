Job Pre-Check
  • Verify site ready within 2 days
  • Site graded, backfilled, safe
  • Temp power available
  • Foundation ready
  • Plans in permit box
  • Measure pony walls + send to sales
  • Confirm subcontractor readiness
  • DO NOT call – use Appwright for scheduling
Pre-Construction Meeting
  • Redline plans with builder
  • Upload redline plans in Appwright
  • Verify W/D openings
  • Confirm truss + stair delivery
  • Identify change orders
  • Walk foundation with framer
Framing Start
  • Block foundation
  • Open floor inspection required
  • Coordinate inspection with builder
  • Slab must be square + level
  • Document issues in Appwright
PM Duties During Construction
  • Daily jobsite visits required
  • Update Appwright schedule
  • Daily safety checks
  • Communicate with subcontractor, builder, sales
  • All material requests in Appwright (WHY + WHAT)
Safety Inspections
  • 1 per job minimum
  • 3 per week / 12 per month / 36 per quarter
  • Photos + notes REQUIRED
  • PM + subcontractor signature required
First Walk Framing
  • Must be completed before framer leaves
  • Check ALL window & door openings
  • Compare field vs plans
  • Mark deficiencies with paint
  • Required before 90% payout
Material Return
  • Stack materials neatly
  • Upload photos
  • Send Appwright message → Sales + Framing
Second Walk Framing
  • Re-mark missed items
  • Identify trade damage
  • Extra work requires approval BEFORE starting
  • Check bowed studs
Framer Extras
  • Must be approved BEFORE work starts
  • Submit in Appwright
  • Builder PO required
  • No approval = risk of no pay
Job Closeout
  • Final inspection passed
  • Upload inspection reports
  • Install goal posts
  • Triggers final 10% payout
Key Policies
  • No framing during severe weather
  • No truss install during high winds
  • All backcharges documented in Appwright
  • All builder communication should be documented
